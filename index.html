How to Play

Pokémon Memory Match is a game where you have to match all the Pokémon cards in the least amount of time and turns possible. The game ends when all the cards are matched.

To keep track of your progress, check your stats bar: Timer: Shows the time elapsed since the game started. Turns: Shows the number of turns you've taken. Score: Shows the score you've just achieved.

To play, click on a card to reveal the Pokémon. Then, click on another card to reveal another Pokémon. If the Pokémon match, they will stay turned over. If they don't match, they will be hidden again. Keep playing until all the Pokémon are revealed.

Card background colours represent the Pokémon type; for example purple for a poison type Pokémon, pink for a psychic type.
