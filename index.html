How to Play

Pokémon Memory Match is a game where you have to match all the Pokémon cards in the least amount of time and turns possible. The game ends when all the cards are matched.

To play, click on a card to reveal the Pokémon. Then, click on another card to reveal the Pokémon. If the Pokémon match, they will stay revealed. If they don't match, they will be hidden again. Keep playing until all the Pokémon are revealed.
